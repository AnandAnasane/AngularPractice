Pipe in Angular

Default pipe and Custom Pipe

Default Pipes

UpperCase Pipe

Original Value: - {{firstText}}
After Pipe: - {{firstText | uppercase}}

LowerCase Pipe

Original Value: - {{firstText}}
After Pipe: - {{firstText | lowercase}}

TitleCase Pipe

Original Value: - {{thirdText}}
After Pipe: - {{thirdText | titlecase}}

Date pipe

Original Value: - {{currentDate}}
After Pipe: - {{currentDate | date}}

Date pipe diff type

Original Value: - {{currentDate}}
After Pipe: - {{currentDate | date:'dd-mm-yy'}}
After Pipe: - {{currentDate | date:'dd-MMM-yy'}}

Currency Pipe

Original Value: - {{currency}}
After Pipe: - {{currency | currency: 'INR'}}

JSON pipe

Original Value: - {{student}}
After Pipe: - {{student | json}}

Async Pipe

Original Value: - {{currentTime}};
After Pipe: -{{currentTime | async}}

Multiple pipes at one time

Execution of pipes left to right
Original Value: - {{currentTime}}
After Pipe: - {{currentTime | async | date:'hh:mm:ss'}}

Custom Pipes

Original Value: - {{employee.state}}
After Pipe: state:-{{employee.state | na}}

Here, we use custom pipes - IF the value of State is null,undefined or empty it will print NA rather than print nothing
